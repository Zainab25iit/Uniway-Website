Law Field

Law, the discipline and profession concerned with the customs, practices, and rules of conduct of a community that are recognized as binding by the community. Enforcement of the body of rules is through a controlling authority. A law degree is a gateway to a career as a solicitor or barrister, but it's not the only path you can choose.
