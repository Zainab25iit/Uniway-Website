Psychology

Psychology is one of the most popular subjects to study at degree level. Psychology looks at the ways people think, act, react, and interact.
It is the study of human (and animal) behaviour, and the thoughts and emotions that influence behaviour. This is important if you want to pursue a career as a practicing psychologist with chartered status.

Popular Psychology Types

  • Child psychology
  • Clinical psychology
  • Educational psychology
  • Experimental psychology
  • Forensic psychology
  • Sport psychology
  • General Psychology
