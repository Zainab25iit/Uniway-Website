Business Management

Business Management refers to the procedure of arranging, directing and overseeing a company's or organization's acitivities in order toward achieving its objectives and goals.
It entails being in charge of all parts of a company, fromFinancial Management and Operations to the human resources and marketing departments.
Business Management's major purpose is to optimize the organization'sperfomance,profits and sustainability over time.
Managers at all levels are critical in makingdecisions,developing strategies,assigning resources,and managing regular tasks to achieve desired results.
